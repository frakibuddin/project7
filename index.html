about us

We are dedicated to serve the rubber industries with alternative rubber raw material which we believe that it could replace the prime grade of the raw material. Our company is specialized in grading and trading of alternative rubber raw material (specifically NBR) which is reprocessed from the off-standard/off-grade rubber and turn it back into usable pure virgin raw material.

Our alternative NBR raw material are neither reclaimed nor recycled, they are reprocessed, which means it have the purest state of the properties without adding any addictive or agents. It is reprocessed from the off-standard/off-grade rubber and turns it back to usable alternative raw material.

Johns Traders LLP is having tie-ups with well-equipped testing laboratories both in private and Govt sectors for testing raw-material and finished product, rubber compound based on reclaim or reprocessed Rubber. These substitute rubber is manufactured with the latest technology under the guidance of highly experienced trained technicians.
